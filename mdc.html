Matrices and Differential Calculus (MDC)

Foundational mathematics for engineering and problem solving

Subject Overview

Matrices and Differential Calculus (MDC) is a core mathematical subject for engineering students. It provides essential tools for modeling, analyzing, and solving complex engineering problems, including systems of equations, transformations, and rate-of-change calculations.

Importance of MDC

Understanding matrices allows students to solve linear systems, perform transformations, and analyze networks. Differential calculus equips learners with the ability to understand change, optimize solutions, and model real-world phenomena across engineering disciplines.
