Computer Science

Foundations of computation, logic, and problem solving

Subject Overview

Computer Science introduces students to the core principles of computing systems, algorithms, data representation, and programming logic. It builds analytical thinking and provides a strong foundation for advanced technical subjects.

Why Computer Science Matters

Computer Science is the backbone of modern technology. From software development to artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, the concepts learned here enable students to understand how systems work internally and how efficient solutions are designed.
