Applied Chemistry (AC)

Practical understanding of chemical principles for engineering applications

Subject Overview

Applied Chemistry focuses on the fundamental chemical principles and their applications in engineering and technology. It covers the study of materials, chemical reactions, solutions, and instrumentation techniques relevant to engineering problems.

Importance of AC

Applied Chemistry equips students with knowledge to select and utilize chemical materials effectively in engineering applications. It enhances laboratory skills, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities crucial for multidisciplinary engineering work.
