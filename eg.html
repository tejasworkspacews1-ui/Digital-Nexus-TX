Engineering Graphics (EG)

Visualizing concepts, precision, and technical drafting

Subject Overview

Engineering Graphics is the foundation for visualizing and representing engineering ideas through technical drawings and diagrams. It develops spatial understanding, precision, and the ability to convey design intent through standardized drafting techniques.

Importance of EG

EG trains students to interpret and create accurate drawings for components, assemblies, and systems. Strong visualization skills gained here are crucial for mechanical, civil, and electrical disciplines alike.
