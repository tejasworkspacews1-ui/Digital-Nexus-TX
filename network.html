Network Analysis

Understanding electrical networks with precision and clarity

Subject Overview

Network Analysis is a fundamental subject in Electrical and Electronics Engineering that teaches students how to analyze, model, and predict the behavior of electrical circuits. It focuses on both DC and AC networks, transient and steady-state analysis, and the application of various theorems and techniques for simplifying complex circuits.

Importance of Network Analysis

A solid understanding of network analysis is essential for designing efficient electrical systems, troubleshooting faults, and building simulation models. It equips students with analytical thinking abilities and prepares them for advanced subjects like Power Systems, Signal Processing, and Control Systems.
