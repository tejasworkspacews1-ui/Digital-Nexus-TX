Request Access

Secure, ethical, and responsible sharing of academic resources

Why Request Access?

All experiment documents hosted on Digital Nexus are protected to ensure academic integrity. Open distribution is restricted to prevent misuse, plagiarism, and unauthorized commercial usage.

By requesting access, students confirm that their usage will remain educational, ethical, and aligned with university guidelines.

Access Request Process

  1. Click the request button below.
  2. Fill out the Google Form or external request portal.
  3. Provide your academic details for verification.
  4. Wait for manual approval from the platform administrator.
  5. Once approved, you will receive a private download link.

This manual review ensures only genuine academic users gain access, preserving the credibility and purpose of the platform.

Submit Your Request

Click the button below to submit your access request via a trusted external form. Ensure all details are accurate for faster approval.

Ethical Usage

Approved users are expected to:

  • ✔ Use documents solely for personal academic purposes
  • ✔ Avoid sharing or distributing downloaded materials
  • ✔ Respect intellectual property and academic integrity
  • ✔ Provide feedback or corrections if discrepancies are found
