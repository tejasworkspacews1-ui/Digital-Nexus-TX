Applied Physics (AP)

Exploring the principles of physics applied to engineering

Subject Overview

Applied Physics emphasizes the practical understanding of physics concepts and their applications in engineering. It covers mechanics, optics, electricity, magnetism, and modern physics topics essential for laboratory and real-world engineering scenarios.

Importance of AP

Applied Physics builds foundational skills to analyze and solve engineering problems. Laboratory experiments teach measurement precision, observation, data analysis, and correlation of theory with practice.
